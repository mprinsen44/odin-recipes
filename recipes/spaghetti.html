Homemade Spaghetti

Back to Homepage

Make sure to have a well salted pot of boiling water ready

The following are a list of ingredients to make the spaghetti

  • Kosher Salt
  • Ground Hamburger
  • Cloves of Garlic
  • Onion Powder or Fresh onions
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Parmasean
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Add noodles to the water
  2. Get your skillet nice and hot and add oil
  3. Add your ground beef to the skillet
  4. Once mostly brown remove the access oil and water
  5. Season the ground beef to your liking
  6. Add sauce to the meat
  7. Add the parmesean
  8. Strain the noodles and add to meat sauce
  9. Bon appetit!
