New York Strip Steak

Back to Mainpage

Purchase a thick cut of New York Striploin steak at your local butcher or supermarket

The following are a list of ingredients to season the steak with:

  • Kosher Salt
  • Butter
  • Cloves of Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the steak with kosher salt to your liking
  2. Get your skillet nice and hot and add oil
  3. Sear the steak on both sides a minute per side
  4. Add cloves of Garlic and thyme and rosemary
  5. Add butter and continue to baste the steak
  6. Use thermometer to determine how cooked you like the steak
  7. Season the steak with black pepper before plating
  8. Let the steak rest for at least 5 minutes prior to cutting
  9. Bon appetit!
